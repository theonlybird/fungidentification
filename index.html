Welcome to the Mushroom ID Challenge!

You can choose a quiz of 10, 20 or 30 questions. Try to identify as many fungi as you can from the 4 multiple-choice answers.

There will be 2–4 photos in each round (taken from an iNaturalist observation). If the photos are too poor in quality, you can click New Photos to load another observation of the same species.

There are four difficulty levels: a friendly Beginner mode with a small set of iconic species, then Novice, Intermediate, and Expert which have a much larger pool of fungi with increasingly difficult multiple choice options.

Per 10 questions, Beginners get 3 New Photos uses, Novices get 2 and Intermediates/Experts get 1.

iNaturalist is a wonderful citizen-science website, but not all observations are guaranteed to be correct. This quiz is intended to be a fun learning tool so should be treated as more of a guide than gospel.

You can review all of your answers and the observations at the end. Good luck!
